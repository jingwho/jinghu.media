working together — outside of a newsletter

I work with a small number of clients each year. Three things I do:

Speaking. Events and offsites for teams and leaders who need a clear-eyed read on where AI is actually going, and where the coverage is getting it over the top. Available for the right event.

Commissioned research. The same analysis I do publicly, pointed at a specific question you need answered privately. Published under your name or kept as an internal memo.

Advisory. Ongoing relationships with a small number of funds, operators, and leadership teams who want a second brain on AI bets — what to buy, what to build, what to ignore. Usually a monthly retainer with a call cadence and ad-hoc memos.
